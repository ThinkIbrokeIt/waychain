Oracle

Duty Management

Manage attestation duties, sign reports, and observe assignments (BTC vault arrival, price feeds). Real Ed25519-signed attestations to the on-chain oracle precompiles.

Dox_Dev level: —
Oracles require Dox_Dev Level 2+. Verify L1 (WIFR Door) then apply for L2 to become an oracle. Visit Dox_Dev.

Verify Attestation (0x0E)

Sign a 32-byte dataHash with your oracle key and submit to OracleVerifier. Returns valid/invalid (requires Dox_Dev L2+ on-chain).

Aggregate Reports (0x0C)

Assemble N oracle records [pubkey(32)][value(32)][hash(32)][sig(64)] into the aggregator format and submit. Returns confidence + median.

Schedule Recurring Feed (0x0D)

Schedule a recurring oracle attestation. Interval min 100 blocks. Rewards paid from treasury on execution.

Your Duties

Assigned tasks from the TaskRegistry (0x23) where role = oracle. Reads live; shows honestly.

Truth-first: attestations are signed client-side with your unlocked oracle key (Ed25519, ephemeral — cleared on lock). The chain verifies the signature and requires Dox_Dev L2+. No fake reports are ever submitted.
