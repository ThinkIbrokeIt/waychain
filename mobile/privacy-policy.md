# WayChain Wallet — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-07-15

WayChain Wallet is a self-custody cryptocurrency wallet. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is handled.

## 1. What we do NOT collect
WayChain Wallet is non-custodial. We do **not** collect, store, or transmit:
- Your private keys, recovery phrase, or seed
- Your wallet address or transaction history
- Any personally identifiable information (name, email, phone)

All keys are generated and stored **on your device only**. We cannot reset your password or recover your keys.

## 2. On-chain data
Using the wallet requires reading and writing public blockchain data (balances, transactions, validator/oracle status) via public WayChain RPC nodes. This data is public by design and not controlled by us.

## 3. Third-party services
- The app connects to public WayChain RPC endpoints to query chain state and broadcast transactions.
- Push notifications (if enabled) use the device's standard notification service.
- We use no analytics SDKs, no advertising identifiers, and no third-party trackers.

## 4. Permissions
- **Internet** — required to reach WayChain nodes.
- **Camera** (optional) — only to scan QR codes for addresses; images are processed on-device and never uploaded.

## 5. Children
The wallet is not directed to anyone under 18 and must not be used by minors.

## 6. Changes
This policy may change as features evolve. The current version is always published at https://waychain.org/privacy.
